Store

Most of my work is available to purchase on this site. Just send me an email and tell me which painting you would like to buy.

Payment Methods

I accept the following payment methods:

  • Online bank transfer
  • Certified cheques
  • Money orders
  • Personal cheques
  • Cash (if you live in the Lower Mainland)

Return, Refund and Cancellation Policy

If your purchase is damaged in the mail, please notify me by email, cferancik@gmail.com, and include a description of the damage along with a photo, if possible. Keep all the packaging material intact, as you will most likely have to ship it back for repair or replacement.

I offer a seven-day return policy. If you change your mind after receiving your goods, you have seven days to notify me by email, cferancik@gmail.com. You will need to fund the return shipping to return the piece. Please make sure you package the piece the best way that you can in the original packaging material. You will get a full refund when I receive the painting.

Shipping

Standard shipping fees apply to Canada and the USA. I offer free shipping on orders that exceed $500 (based on multiple purchases in one order, paintings need to be shipped at the same time). Please email me for costs on international shipping.

Custom Orders

I welcome custom art commissions of any kind — paintings, drawings, sculpture, mixed media, etc. Please complete the contact form to outline the details of your commission, along with your budget and dimensions and I will follow up with you on pricing, timeline and expectations.
